The Best Movies According to Akshitha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Alai Payuthey

Alai Payuthey is a 2000 Indian Tamil-language romantic drama film written, co-produced and directed by Mani Ratnam.

Arjun Reddy

Arjun Reddy is a 2017 Indian Telugu-language romantic drama film written and directed by Sandeep Reddy Vanga.

Fifty Shades Of Grey

Fifty Shades of Grey is a 2015 American erotic romantic drama film directed by Sam Taylor-Johnson.
